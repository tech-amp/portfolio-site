About us

Hi! We are Durval and Pete: a group of freelancers setting out on a journey! Our dream is to work on projects that we love, cultivate our skills every day, and meet amazing people along the way!

We have a passion for design and development, and are confident that we can help you with our insights. A main component of our work process is planning business strategy, social media marketing, and digital impression to capture leads for your project.

Social Media

Our main area of expertise is in web development. This covers the basics (HTML5, CSS3, JS/jQuery) of course, but we also have experience in back end development using Python and PHP. We can use a a variety of frameworks, including Angular and Django.

Another area we can help you with is building a web-site on an existing CMS (Content Management System) such as Wordpress, Wix, etc. Some clients prefer to use a CMS as it enables intuitive content addition after the site has been created!
